MASER

A tool for scheduling time series acquisition of radio emission from magnetic interactions between stars and planets. Enter the physical parameters of the system along with your observing schedule and the visibility windows of the signal from the Northern and Southern magnetic hemispheres of the star will be displayed. The time series data shown in the figure can also be downloaded via the button below. For more details, see the MASER repository on GitHub.
