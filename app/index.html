MASER

A tool for scheduling time series acquisition of radio emission from magnetic interactions between stars and planets. Enter the physical parameters of the system along with your observing schedule and the visibility windows of the signal from the Northern and Southern magnetic hemispheres of the star will be computed. The time series data can be downloaded via the button below the figure.
